Which of these is NOT a type of machine learning?
Transcendental Learning is not a recognized type of machine learning. The established types include Supervised Learning (training with labeled data), Unsupervised Learning (finding patterns in unlabeled data), and Reinforcement Learning (learning through rewards/penalties).
What does the acronym 'GPT' stand for in AI models like GPT-4?
GPT stands for 'Generative Pre-trained Transformer.' It refers to a neural network architecture that's been pre-trained on vast amounts of text data and can generate human-like text based on the input it receives.
Which company developed the deep learning system AlphaGo, which defeated the world champion at the board game Go?
AlphaGo was developed by DeepMind, a company acquired by Google. In 2016, AlphaGo defeated Lee Sedol, the 18-time world champion, marking a significant milestone in AI development.
What is a neural network in AI designed to mimic?
Neural networks are designed to mimic the human brain's network of neurons. They consist of interconnected nodes (artificial neurons) that process and transmit information, allowing the system to learn patterns and make decisions.
Which of these is a primary ethical concern related to AI development?
Algorithmic bias is a major ethical concern in AI development. When AI systems are trained on biased data, they can perpetuate or amplify existing societal biases, leading to unfair outcomes for certain groups of people.
