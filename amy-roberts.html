Sentence Formation Practice

Instructions:

In this activity, you'll practice making sentences, questions, and negatives using information about Amy Roberts. Look at Amy's information card and complete the exercises below.

Name Amy Roberts
Country England
Address 18, Market Street, Manchester
Phone number 0161 929 5837
Age 20
Job Student
Married? No

Part 1: Making Sentences

Drag and drop the words to make correct sentences about Amy.

1. Make a sentence about Amy's name:

Amy Roberts is name Her

2. Make a sentence about where Amy is from:

Amy from England is

3. Make a sentence about Amy's age:

is She years old 20

Part 2: Making Questions

Drag and drop the words to form questions about Amy.

1. Make a question to ask about Amy's name:

is What name her ?

2. Make a question to ask about Amy's country:

Where is she from ?

3. Make a question to ask about Amy's job:

What is her job ?

4. Make a question to ask if Amy is married:

Is she married ?

Part 3: Making Negative Sentences

Complete the sentences with the correct negative form.

1. Amy is NOT from France:

Amy from France.

2. Amy is NOT married:

Amy married.

3. Amy does NOT work as a doctor:

Amy work as a doctor.

4. Amy is NOT 25 years old:

Amy 25 years old.

Part 4: Mixed Practice

Fill in the blanks with the correct words to complete the sentences, questions or negatives about Amy.

1. Amy a student.

2. Amy from England?

3. She not live in London.

4. old is Amy?

Great Job!

What you've learned:

  • How to make basic sentences about a person
  • How to form questions to ask for information
  • How to make negative sentences

Remember:

Affirmative sentences: Subject + verb + rest of sentence

Questions: Question word (What, Where, How) + auxiliary verb (is, does) + subject + rest

Negatives: Subject + auxiliary verb + not + rest of sentence
